Books I've read recently, with quick thoughts.

 - ## **Atomic Habits** — *James Clear*

    ![Atomic Habits cover](https://covers.openlibrary.org/b/isbn/9780735211292-M.jpg)

    Small changes, remarkable results. The core idea is that 1% improvements compound over time. The "habit stacking" technique — linking a new habit to an existing one — actually stuck with me. Practical and actionable. [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0735211299)


 - ## **Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind** — *Yuval Noah Harari*

   ![Sapiens cover](https://covers.openlibrary.org/b/isbn/9780062316097-M.jpg)
   
   A sweeping look at how *Homo sapiens* came to dominate the planet. The "cognitive revolution" chapter — about how shared myths (religion, money, nations) allowed humans to cooperate at scale — was the most thought-provoking part. [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0062316095)


 - ## **The Alchemist** — *Paulo Coelho*

   ![The Alchemist cover](https://covers.openlibrary.org/b/isbn/9780062315007-M.jpg)

   A shepherd boy's journey to find treasure near the pyramids. It's really about listening to your heart and recognizing opportunities. Short, simple, and the kind of book you think about weeks later. [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0062315005)


 - ## **Thinking, Fast and Slow** — *Daniel Kahneman*

   ![Thinking, Fast and Slow cover](https://covers.openlibrary.org/b/isbn/9780374533557-M.jpg)

   System 1 (fast, intuitive) vs System 2 (slow, deliberate). Explains why we make irrational decisions even when we know better. Dense but rewarding — I keep coming back to the anchoring bias chapter. [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0374533555)


 - ## **Project Hail Mary** — *Andy Weir*

   ![Project Hail Mary cover](https://covers.openlibrary.org/b/isbn/9780593135204-M.jpg)

   A lone astronaut wakes up on a spaceship with no memory and has to save humanity. If you liked *The Martian*, you'll love this — same humor, bigger stakes. The alien friendship is genuinely heartwarming. [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0593135202)
